# GoCarte FAQ

## Overview

This FAQ addresses common questions about using GoCarte. For detailed instructions and troubleshooting guidance, refer to the other sections of the GoCarte Product Documentation.

---

## What is a Workroom?

A Workroom is the primary collaborative space in GoCarte. Workrooms contain projects, tasks, and the team members assigned to them. Each Workroom represents a group of users working together on related initiatives.

---

## What is a Workroom Sandbox?

A Workroom Sandbox is a collaborative drafting space within a Workroom where team members can experiment with project plans, notes, or task structures before committing them to the main project workflow.

Sandboxes allow teams to co-author materials and explore project ideas without affecting active tasks or project timelines.

Workroom Sandboxes can also be shared across multiple Workrooms, allowing users from different teams to collaborate on the same project content.

---

## What is the Task Management Container?

The Task Management Container is the navigation panel where projects and their associated tasks are displayed. It allows users to view project structures, expand or collapse task groups, access archived or completed tasks, and quickly access tasks within a Workroom.

Projects appear as the top-level items in the container, with tasks and subtasks nested beneath them.

---

## Can a user belong to multiple Workrooms?

Yes. A user can belong to multiple Workrooms if they are granted access by a Workroom administrator. Permissions determine which Workrooms a user can view and manage.

---

## What is the difference between a project and a task?

Projects organize work related to a specific initiative, such as a marketing campaign or product release.

Tasks represent individual units of work associated with a project. Tasks can include descriptions, due dates, and assigned team members.

---

## Can tasks be duplicated across projects?

Yes. Tasks can be duplicated when the same work applies to multiple projects. This allows teams to reuse recurring tasks or workflows without recreating them manually.

Tasks can also be duplicated within a Workroom Sandbox and then copied to another Workroom if collaboration across teams is required.

---

## What happens when a task is unassigned?

Unassigned tasks remain visible within the project task list but are not attached to a specific team member. Tasks can be assigned later when responsibility for the work is determined.

Tasks can also be assigned to **All team members** by the task creator.

Completed tasks are archived and searchable in the Task Management Container.

---

## How is project progress tracked?

Project progress is automatically tracked based on the status of tasks within the project. As tasks move from **To Do** to **In Progress** and **Completed**, the project dashboard updates to reflect overall progress.

---

## Why can't I access a Workroom?

Access to a Workroom depends on user permissions. If you cannot view a Workroom, confirm that you are signed in with the correct account and that the Workroom administrator has granted you access.

---

## How often do passwords expire?

For security purposes, GoCarte passwords must be reset every 90 days. Users will receive a notification prompting them to update their password two weeks before it expires.

If a password expires, the account must be restored by the IT Help Desk.

---
