# Resetting Your GoCarte Password

## Overview

If you cannot access your GoCarte account because you lost or forgot your password, you can reset it using the password recovery option on the login page.

Resetting your password sends a secure verification link to the email address associated with your account. After verification, you can create a new password and sign back in.

Passwords must also be reset every 90 days to prevent loss of access to your account. Forgotten passwords can be reset by the user. Expired passwords require authorization from the IT Help Desk.

---

## Reset Your Password

Follow these steps to reset your GoCarte password.

1. Go to the GoCarte login page.  
2. Select **Forgot Password**.  
3. Enter the email address associated with your GoCarte account.  
4. Select **Send Reset Link**.  
5. Open the password reset email sent to your inbox.  
6. Select the **Reset Password** link in the email.  
7. When redirected to GoCarte, enter the **two-factor authentication (2FA)** code sent to the phone number associated with your account.  
8. Enter a new password.  
9. Select **Confirm Password Reset**.  

When redirected to the GoCarte landing page, sign in using your new password.

---

## If You Do Not Receive the Reset Email

If the password reset email does not appear in your inbox, check the following:

- Confirm that you entered the correct email address.  
- Check your spam or junk mail folder.  
- Wait a few minutes and request another reset link.  
- Make sure the password reset link has not expired. Password reset links expire after five minutes.

If you still do not receive the reset email, contact your GoCarte administrator for assistance or submit a ticket to the IT Help Desk.
